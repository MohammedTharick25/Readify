About Readify

Our mission is to connect readers with their next favorite book

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Readify began as a small team of book enthusiasts who wanted to create a better way to discover and purchase eBooks. We noticed that existing platforms were either too impersonal or too focused on bestsellers, making it difficult for readers to find hidden gems.

Today, Readify has grown into a leading eBook marketplace with thousands of titles across all genres. We're proud to support independent authors and publishers while providing readers with an exceptional browsing and reading experience.

Our Mission

At Readify, we believe that books have the power to transform lives. Our mission is to:

  • Make reading more accessible to everyone
  • Help readers discover books they'll love
  • Support authors and publishers at all stages of their careers
  • Create a community of passionate readers

Why Choose Readify?

With so many options for buying eBooks, here's what makes us different:

Curated Selection

Our team handpicks books to ensure quality and variety in every genre. We go beyond just the bestsellers to highlight emerging authors and unique voices.

Reader-First Approach

We design every feature with readers in mind, from personalized recommendations to our easy-to-use reading app that syncs across all your devices.

Fair Pricing

We work directly with publishers to offer competitive prices and frequent discounts, making great books more affordable.
